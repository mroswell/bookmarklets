Map a Congressional Roll Call Vote (Bookmarklet)

This bookmarklet enables prettier House and Senate roll call vote view.
  • Install the bookmarklet by dragging this link to your browser's bookmark toolbar. (Ensure that the toolbar is showing first.)

    MapVote

  • Visit http://thomas.loc.gov/home/rollcallvotes.html, and drill down to a detail roll call vote page, in the format:
    http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2015/roll374.xml
    or
    http://www.senate.gov/legislative/LIS/roll_call_lists/roll_call_vote_cfm.cfm?congress=114&session=1&vote=00101
  • Press your new 'MapVote' button, to be brought to the beautiful New York Times Congress API view of the vote, including maps, colors, etc.
